About Marcus

Marcus Fields studies professional writing at Michigan State University. He is a student in the Residential College in the Arts and Humanities, the Honors College, and he studies arts and cultural management through the College of Arts and Letters. When Marc isn't studying, he's acting or directing with his friends through the Roial Players, a student-led theater group at Michigan State.
